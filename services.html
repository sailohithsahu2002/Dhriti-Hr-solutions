Recruiting, Hiring and Retaining Talent

Talent acquisition is one of the core functions of Dhriti HR Solutions. Effectively assessing the workforce capabilities and performance to match organizational goals is crucial for the health of the organization. HR managers have to keep their finger on the pulse of the company to ensure personnel levels can meet demand. They must also be strategic when hiring for new roles or restructuring teams and make hard decisions about staffing.

Recruiting Candidates

Finding the right person starts with accurate and detailed job descriptions. Dhriti analyzes the market and sets competitive salary ranges before promoting available positions on various platforms. We screen applications and resumes to identify which candidates should move forward in the hiring process. From there, we shortlist the most desired and eligible candidates and simplify the entire recruitment process.

Hiring Employees

Dhriti conducts thorough background checks, manages onboarding of new employees, and explains payroll, benefits and company policies. There is considerable important legal paperwork, recordkeeping and administrative responsibility involved in the hiring process. We ensure all information is present, correct and properly protected, which is critical to the security of employees and the company.

Retaining Talent

Dhriti is also responsible for employee retention, which is often overlooked in discussions around talent acquisition. We help maintain these key elements:

  • Respectful treatment of all employees at all levels
  • Trust between employees and senior management
  • Opportunities to use skills and abilities at work
  • Fair compensation and competitive pay
  • Job security

Employee Engagement

The only way to build and maintain a vibrant company culture is to unite everyone around shared goals, mission and values. Dhriti encourages employees to discuss concerns or report situations so that problems can be worked through as soon as possible, instead of snowballing into a potentially damaging situation.

Building Rapport and Trust

The following activities help build strong relationships among leadership, management and employees:

  • Recognizing individual and group achievements
  • Sharing successes and failures transparently
  • Asking for feedback and listening to concerns
  • Organizing company-wide gatherings and team events
  • Communicating new policies, decisions and strategic goals in a timely manner
  • Mediating conflicts or tensions between employees

Performance Management

Performance management is the process of maintaining or improving job performance. Typically, this is done through the use of assessment tools, coaching and counselling, and providing continuous feedback. This systematic approach helps employees understand expectations and achieve organizational objectives while developing professionally.

Compensation and Benefits

Calculating fair compensation rates and putting together a comprehensive benefits package is a significant focus of Dhriti. We ensure every employee receives decent compensation in exchange for their hard work. The goal is to create a competitive and attractive offer that aligns with the candidate's experience and expectations, which is essential for retaining talent in a competitive market.

Development and Training

Offering ample opportunities for individuals to learn new skills and further improve their existing talents is advantageous to both the company and employees alike. Robust professional development programs have positive effects on employee productivity, enhance performance, increase opportunities to promote from within, and reduce turnover rates. When employees feel their organization is investing in their growth, they are more likely to engage with and shape company culture positively.

Training Program Focus Areas

Dhriti HR Solutions provides training sessions aligned with the organization's strategic goals and legal responsibilities. Our team coordinates company-wide informational sessions and seminars around important topics including:

  • Diversity, inclusion and equity in the workplace
  • Recognizing and reporting sexual harassment
  • Mandated reporting requirements
  • Emerging technology and digital skills
  • Industry and market trends

Risk Management

Another key function of human resources is identifying, assessing and resolving short- and long-term risks before they become a real threat. This serves to protect both the employees and the company. While risk management isn't typically the sole responsibility of HR professionals, they play an important role in the process.

Risk Mitigation Strategies

Some examples of how Dhriti HR department mitigates organizational risk include:

  • Properly training managers and collecting anonymous feedback from their direct reports
  • Implementing measures to maintain or increase workplace diversity
  • Working with IT to ensure data and sensitive records are secure and private
  • Accurately tracking, updating and protecting employee records
  • Investigating complaints and allegations of harassment, racism, sexism, favoritism, etc.

Audits and Legal Compliance

The laws on matters such as labour, benefits, fair compensation, hiring and firing are complex. They also vary by state and are subject to change periodically. Knowing and following state, federal and local regulations is necessary for the success of any organization.

Dhriti HR Solutions remains up-to-date with all regulatory requirements and ensures your organization maintains full compliance across all legal frameworks, protecting both your business and your employees.
